Teacakes list of top movies

Top 3 movies of all time

1st. The impossible

Adventure/Drama
2012
1h 54m

Genuinely one of the most harrowing and heart breaking films I have ever watched. Based on the true events of a devastating tsunami that hit the coast of Thailand.

2nd. Se7en

Crime/Mystery
1995
2h 7m

Who doesn't love a good murder mysetery, this gruesome and twisted depiction of what the human mind is capabale of is probably what got me hooked on crime novels as a young adult.

3rd. Hereditary

Horror/Indie film
2018
2h 7m

Given that I am not a horror movie fan, to see this on my top 3 probably tells you just how good a film it is. The geniuousness of the films cinematography creates the most eerie atmosphere you can image that will have you both struggling to look away but wishing you could.
